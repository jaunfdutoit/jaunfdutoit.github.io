ENVIRONMENT

Modulism is confined to the digital realm, where it lives in a world of endless possibilities. Modulism is a UI design language created to accommodate the contemporary UI designer to serve as a guideline for creating web experiences. Modulism embraces the flatness and slimness of the latest device screens in the world today, by not relying on a 3-dimensional perception of the digital sphere. The principles of Modulism are in place to ensure this environment is utilitised in its full potential.

Modulism is based on the characteristics of Modernism, to serve a function of creating functional and aesthetically pleasing User interfaces from mobile to desktop. Modulism taking a standpoint of having a mobile first approach slightly different to the execution of Material design, should be used only where a mobile presence is required.

PROPERTIES

A module is a flat element that serves as a container to hold the content of the UI, such as buttons, images, and text.

A module is considered to be a rectangular shape, which does not have rounded corners or contrasting borders.

Modules are confined to the flatness of the screen it is displayed and should not rise from or sink into the depth of the screen.
