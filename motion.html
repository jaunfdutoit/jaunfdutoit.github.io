MOTION

When designing for the web one must consider the spectrum of devices that need to be catered for. It is non-beneficial to the experience if one designs for a device that can handle a high frame rate as opposed to a device that, because of its hardware and/or software capabilities cannot handle high frame rates. Material design makes use of authentic motion that requires high frame rates to work efficiently. Modulism striving to focus on function attempts to standardise the type of motion applied on UIs. Subtle motion can be just as affective as authentic motion.

Subtle motion application:

  • Buttons
  • Menus
  • Drop downs
  • Hover effects on content (eg. images)

BASIC MOTION

A module is a flat element that serves as a container to hold the content of the UI, such as buttons, images, and text.
